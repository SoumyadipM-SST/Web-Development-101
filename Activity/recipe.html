Delicious Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purposes flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt
  2. In another bowl, beat egg with milk and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingedients and mix until smooth.
  4. Heat a skillet and pour in batter to form pancakes.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, then flip aand cook until golden.

Enjoy your homemade pancakes!
